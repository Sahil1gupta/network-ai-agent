RUNBOOK: POWER_FAULT — Power Supply Failure
=============================================
Severity: CRITICAL
Affected: All services at affected site if single PSU

SYMPTOMS:
- SNMP power supply alarm active
- PSU status LED red
- Event log shows PSU failure

IMMEDIATE STEPS:
1. Check how many PSUs device has, N+1 or single
2. If N+1: second PSU taking load, non-service impacting
3. If single PSU: device at risk, treat as URGENT
4. Check power input: verify commercial power healthy
5. Check UPS battery level

COMMON ROOT CAUSES:
- PSU hardware failure
- Commercial power outage
- UPS battery exhaustion
- Overload due to added hardware

RESOLUTION:
- Replace failed PSU from spare parts inventory
- For single PSU: prepare for emergency failover
- If commercial power issue: coordinate with facilities team
- If UPS failure: dispatch electrician immediately

MTTR Target:
- N+1 setup: 4 hours preventive replacement
- Single PSU with spare: 1 hour
- No spare: escalate to procurement immediately
